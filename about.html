About Me

Why I'm Doing This

I've never been good at basketball. I had a hoop in my driveway, and I went through a period of about a year in high school where I would religiously throw up shots. I got pretty good at it too. I started close to the basket, then would work my way out, throwing the ball off the wall to simulate passes from my teammates, trusting me to take the game winner. They went in more often than not. But, when I stepped on the court all that shooting prowess would disappear, I was a turnstyle on defense, and I had no clue where to be. I quickly determined intramurals were the only place I could play without feeling useless.

But for a sport I had no real aptitude for, boy did I love it. I still can't get enough of it, and try to watch a game a night. This inevitably slows down during the school season, where my studies always seem to get in the way. But after spending so much time reading other people's articles, I thought I should try to make a blog of my own. Odds are, no one will ever see this. My analysis is surface level, and my takes are ice cold. But this is a project for me. For me to enjoy, and look back on, and for me to be proud of. I'm making a commitment to make a prediction for every game for the rest of the 2018-2019 season.

Good predictions?

Maybe not.

In depth predictions?

Definitely not.

But predictions nonetheless. Hopefully I can surprise myself with a few of them.
