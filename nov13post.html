My Picks: Tuesday, November 13th

Posted by Sam Worrod on November 13, 2018

Last Night: 5-4

Game 1: Charlotte Hornets at Cleveland Cavaliers

My Pick:

Charlotte Hornets

Why I'm Rolling with them:

If you were to take the Cavaliers record and extrapolate it over the course of an entire season, they would be the worst team of all time. The Hornets are better than their record suggests, with a staggering 7.6 net rating. That's as good as Toronto is. Tony Parker could be a starter on quite a few teams in the league, and he's producing very well off of the bench for them. Colin Sexton should probably not be starting right now, but theres still a lot of room for growth there. Either way, Charlotte takes it 110-102.

Game 2: Houston Rockets at Denver Nuggets

My Pick:

Denver Nuggets

Why I'm Rolling with them:

The Nuggets have cooled off as of late but I still have faith in their ability to produce against this bottom tier Houston defense. At the end of the day I still have a lot of questions about this Houston roster that I just don't have about the Nuggets, and I think that Capela will have a hard time guarding Jokic. I can see Houston's guards getting a mismatch on him to try and abuse him defensively, but having Millsap as a help defender should be enough for the Nuggets to win this one, 119-113.

James Harden needs to step up if the Rockets want to make a postseason run

Game 3: Atlanta Hawks at Golden State Warriors

My Pick:

Golden State Warriors

Why I'm Rolling with them:

The Warriors lost a close game against the Clippers yesterday that saw them come back from 11 down with 5 minutes left to send the game to overtime before Durant fouled out and the Clippers pulled ahead. This Hawks team is not in the same stratosphere as the Clippers, let alone the Warriors. I would be shocked if the Warriors lost this one, even if they played last night. I have full faith in Durant and Thompson to easily take this one. Warriors, 120-103.
