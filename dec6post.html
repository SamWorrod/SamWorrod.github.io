My Picks: Thursday, December 6th

Posted by Sam Worrod on December 6, 2018

Last Night: 8-2

Game 1: New York Knicks at Boston Celtics

My Pick:

Boston Celtics

Why I'm Rolling with them:

The Celtics should have no trouble with the Knicks, as the Knicks roster has a lot of questions throughout. I'll be interested to see how the team does with Jaylen Brown coming off of the bench, I think this could be a good change for him and allow him to get a greater number of touches compared to when he was starting. The new lineup has a little bit more of an inside presence, at the expense of some perimeter defense. I can see the bench locking up a lot of units for extended periods of time. Boston wins 112-100.

Game 2: Phoenix Suns at Portland Trail Blazers

My Pick:

Portland Trail Blazers

Why I'm Rolling with them:

The Suns are bad. With Booker out they don't have anyone to rely on offensively, and their defensive identity has been suspect all season. The Trail Blazers have been a top tier team both offensively and defensively, and should have no trouble continuing that against a Suns team with no real point guard. Lillard especially should have a field day, as the Suns don't really have a capable defender for him. The Trail Blazers take this one in a blowout, 122-104.

Game 3: Houston Rockets at Utah Jazz

My Pick:

Utah Jazz

Why I'm Rolling with them:

I'm taking the Jazz in this one, they've looked pretty good lately and seem to have figured out some of their early season woes shooting wise. The Rockets currently occupy the 13th seed in the West, having played 2 less games than the Jazz, and are coming off a loss to the Timberwolves that highlighted their deficiencies, namely that the roster needs Harden or Paul to take over a game in order to win. I'm not sure they'll be able to do that tonight, as the Jazz win this one 116-108.
