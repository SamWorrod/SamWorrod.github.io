My Picks: Thursday, December 20th

Posted by Sam Worrod on December 20, 2018

Last Night: 9-3

Game 1: Houston Rockets at Miami Heat

My Pick:

Houston Rockets

Why I'm Rolling with them:

Yes, the Rockets did play yesterday, but they absolutely demolished the Wizards and have finally looked on track. James Harden is playing like an MVP candidate, although I doubt he gets it unless the Rockets manage to win out. Chris Paul has been having crazy high assist numbers too, which should be enlarged tonight as they play a Heat team missing Dragic, who was recently declared out for the next few weeks. This is a game Houston needs to go in and take, and I'm feeling good about their chances. Houston, 119-112.

Game 2: Dallas Mavericks at Los Angeles Clippers

My Pick:

Dallas Mavericks

Why I'm Rolling with them:

After their initial hot start, the Clippers have cooled off considerably, going just 3-7 in their last 10. The Mavericks, 5-5 in their last 10 have played good basketball lately, with Barnes hitting his stride and continuing to support Doncic as this team competes. They have some good perimeter defenders for Lou Will and Shai, which may be enough for them to pull this one out. I also really like the Center matchup for the Mavericks, with Jordan hopefully pulling the Mavs to a 114-110 win.
