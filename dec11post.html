My Picks: Tuesday, December 11th

Posted by Sam Worrod on December 11, 2018

Last Night: 9-2

Game 1: Portland Trail Blazers at Houston Rockets

My Pick:

Portland Trail Blazers

Why I'm Rolling with them:

The Houston Rockets are awful this year, currently holding the 14th seed in the West. The odds of them making the playoffs are getting slimmer by the minute, and that shouldn't change tonight when they go up against the very real, very dangerous Portland Trail Blazers. Although the hype for Portland has died down since the start of the season, they still comfortably sit in the playoffs as the 7 seed. They'll take this one on the back of Lillard, 112-106.

Game 2: Phoenix Suns at San Antonio Spurs

My Pick:

San Antonio Spurs

Why I'm Rolling with them:

The Spurs are just the better team here, and the Suns also played last night in a game that went to overtime. They still don't have Booker, so there's not really anyone to guard DeRozan. Aldridge is also a mismatch. Ayton may actually be able to put up some points tonight, but the Spurs interior defense has been the best attribute of it so far, so I'm expecting big things from them. San Antonio 119-104.

Game 3: Toronto Raptors at Los Angeles Clippers

My Pick:

Los Angeles Clippers

Why I'm Rolling with them:

I'm very scared the Raptors are going to have another three game losing streak, and this game against the Clippers feels very dicey. Leonard is questionable with a bruise, and Lowry has been playing incredibly poorly in the past 4 games. I'm thinking Harris will be able to subdue Siakim more than the majority of power forwards in the league. Because of that, LA takes this one 114-108.
